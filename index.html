Cracked Screen Repair

A shattered display makes your iPhone nearly impossible to use. We replace cracked and broken screens using high-quality OLED or LCD panels that restore original touch sensitivity and colour accuracy.

Battery Replacement

Is your iPhone shutting off unpredictably or barely lasting half a day? We swap out degraded batteries with fresh, certified cells so you get a full day's charge again — usually within the hour.

Charging Port Repair

If your iPhone won't charge consistently or requires a very specific angle to make a connection, the charging port is likely worn or dirty. We clean and replace ports to get you back to reliable charging.

Water Damage Recovery

Dropped your phone in water? Don't wait — time matters. Bring it in as soon as possible and our technicians will disassemble, dry, and clean the internal components to give your iPhone the best chance of recovery.

Speaker & Microphone Issues

Muffled calls or audio that keeps cutting out? We diagnose and replace faulty speakers, earpieces, and microphones so your conversations come through crisp and clear — for you and whoever's on the other end.

Other Hardware Repairs

Broken home button, damaged cameras, Face ID issues, or back glass cracks — if it's a hardware problem, we've likely seen it before and can fix it. Just bring it in for a free assessment.
